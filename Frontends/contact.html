Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support, we aim to respond within 4 hours.

Yes! Our phone support is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM PST. For immediate assistance, you can also use our live chat feature.

Absolutely! We offer personalized demos for educational institutions and organizations. Contact us to schedule a session with our team.
